About Founder Madam, Priti Gupta, who is more than just a teacher

Priti Madam teaches Class 10th Science and Maths subjects as per the curriculum of CBSE board. She is more than just a teacher. She is a dedicated guide who puts her students' learning first. Every class she conducts is well-prepared, engaging, and effective. Her love for teaching reflects in the way she breaks down complex concepts into simple, relatable ideas.

Education wise she is Masters In Computer Applications (MCA) from Rohtak University, Rohtak, Haryana and was working with various IT companies Like Wipro, IBM, etc to name a few. She left her IT job to make carrier in nation building by imparting her knowledge to budding minds.

In Madam Priti's classroom, students are encouraged to actively participate in discussions and ask questions freely. This open learning environment helps build confidence and critical thinking skills.

Interactive activities such as group discussions,development of logical thinking and various problem-solving exercises are a key part of each batch. These methods make the sessions more engaging and help reinforce the concepts taught.

Madam Priti's teaching style is student-centered, focusing on understanding rather than memorization. Her dedication and enthusiasm inspire students to enjoy learning and strive for excellence.
